Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a car rental?▾
Book via our website contact form, call us directly, or WhatsApp. Our team confirms your booking within 30 minutes.
What areas do you cover?▾
We operate across 25+ cities including Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune and many more across India.
What is included in the rental price?▾
The price includes vehicle rent and fuel only. Driver charges, tolls, and GST will be applicable as additional charges.
Can I book for corporate events or long-term contracts?▾
Absolutely! We offer dedicated corporate accounts with monthly billing, SLA guarantees, and a dedicated account manager.
What is your cancellation policy?▾
Free cancellation up to 4 hours before the trip. Cancellations within 4 hours may incur a nominal fee.
Do you provide outstation travel services?▾
Yes! We offer one-way and round-trip outstation services across India with experienced long-distance drivers.
